Positive Impacts:

Some of the positive impacts of Internet technology are: The internet is the guiding technology of IT age. It provides effective communication using emailing or instant messages. It improves in business sectors. Banking and shopping online make life less complicated. We can get free education from internet technology.

Negative Impacts:

Some of the negative impacts Internet Technology are: Easy availability of illegal or inappropriate materials online that is not age suitable. They are affecting mental health, use of technology can have negative repercussions on physical health causing vision problems, hearing loss. People may have sleeping problems and violence.

Conclusions:

In conclusion, Internet technology may have both negative and positive impacts in our daily life. We should use the internet in solving technical issues alongside improving our grammar.
